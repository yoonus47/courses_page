About this Course

athakali (Malayalam: കഥകളി) is a major form of classical Indian dance.[1] It is a "story play" genre of art, but one distinguished by the elaborately colourful make-up, costumes and face masks that the traditionally male actor-dancers wear.[2][3][note 1] Kathakali is a performing art in the Malayalam-speaking southwestern region of Kerala.
